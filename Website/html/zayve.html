ZayvE

Github

What's the easiest way to learn what people around you are interested in?

One way is to use a Google-like platform where you can type in an interest and you get a list of all the people around you with the same interest. Wouldn't that be awesome?

ZayvE is that platform. It's a social media app that is also with various other features to fulfill its dream of being the best platform that connects people with similar interests.

Browse to discover what people around you are interested in!

If you find someone with similar interests as you, Send them a ZayvE by tapping on a matching interest!

Receive ZayvE from people around you who are similar to you

ZayvE requests are sent/received not on the basis of accounts, but on the basis of matching interests!

People are connected when a ZayvE is accepted!

Once connected, people can chat with each other!
